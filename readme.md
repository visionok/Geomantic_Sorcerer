# Geomantic Sorcerer Kit

## Overview

The Geomantic Sorcerer Kit is strongly based on the Druidic Sorcerer Kit by Constant Gaw, and resurrected by hlidskialf and loosely based on the Geomancer prestige class from the AD&D 3rd Edition Masters of the Wild manual. The kit combines the sorcerer's spellcasting methods and spells with additional druidic spells and abilities and features its own unique tiered HLA progression and custom spells. Due to the limitations of the kit selection interface, the kit is not selectable at character creation in original BG2, but if the player meets the kit requirements Aataqah the genie at the start of Chateau Irenicus might see fit to help the player remember their abilities as a geomantic sorcerer. See the kit description below for more details.
